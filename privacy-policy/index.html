Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

How we collect and use information

Shed Design Labs collects information you provide directly, including your name, email, phone number, company details, and any project information submitted through forms, email, or calls.

We use this information to respond to inquiries, deliver services, communicate project updates, send operational notifications, and improve our services.

SMS messaging privacy

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from Shed Design Labs, we may send appointment reminders, project updates, and relevant service communications.

  • SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.
  • Message frequency may vary based on your project activity.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.
  • You can request assistance by replying HELP.

Data sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate our business (for example, hosting, analytics, email, form processing, and messaging platforms) under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your choices

You may request updates or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link when provided.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Shed Design Labs through our contact page.
